About Me

I’m currently a junior at the University of Connecticut pursuing a degree in Digital Media and Design. My focus is in Web Development and Front End Engineering. I first started building websites in highschool, where I used Adobe Dreamweaver to make my first creations. That is where I discovered my love for Digital Media and especially Websites.

Today I code all my websites by hand and use my skills and knowledge to produce content. I’m constantly striving to improve my skills in all aspects of Web Design and am always eager to learn the latest and greatest technology.

When I’m not designing websites, I like to read books (short stories), work on my investment accounts or workout at the gym.
